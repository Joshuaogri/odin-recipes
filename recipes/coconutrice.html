Coconut Rice

What is Coconut rice

Coconut rice is a flavorful and aromatic dish made by cooking rice with coconut milk, giving it a rich and creamy taste. It is a popular dish in many tropical and Asian cuisines.

Some Coconut Rice Ingredients

  • Basmati rice
  • Coconut milk
  • Salt
  • Sunflower oil
  • Shrimps

Steps for cooking coconut rice

  1. Rinse the rice
  2. Cook the rice with coconut milk
  3. Season the rice
  4. Fluff and serve
