Jollof Rice

What is Jollof rice

Jollof rice is a popular African dish predominantly served in the west african part of africa. it is particularly native to Senegal , Nigeria and Ghana where its legendary status as a dish is cemented by the various forms and approach to serving it.Jollof has huge cultural value as an important dish in celebratory and salutary occassions ranging from marriages to birthing ceremonies

Some Jollof Rice Ingredients

  • Basmati rice
  • Three red peppers
  • Curry
  • Thyme
  • Tomato puree

Steps for cooking jollof rice

  1. Gather your ingredients
  2. Prepare the base
  3. Add the spices and rice
  4. Cook the jollof rice
