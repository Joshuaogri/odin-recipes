Fried Rice

What is Fried rice

Fried rice is a popular dish that originated in China but has become a staple in many Asian cuisines and is enjoyed worldwide.

Some Fried Rice Ingredients

  • Basmati rice
  • Shrimps
  • Peas
  • Bell peppers
  • Diced chicken

Steps for cooking Fried rice

  1. Cook the rice
  2. Prepare the Ingredients
  3. Stirfry the Ingredients
  4. Combine and season
